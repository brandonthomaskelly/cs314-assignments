Discover different foods from around the world!

Why France

French cuisine has influenced the style of cooking throughout Europe, and its chefs work in restaurants throughout the world.

Why Canada

Canadian food has been shaped and impacted by continual waves of immigration, with the types of foods and from different regions and periods of Canada reflecting this immigration.

Why Mexico

Mexican cuisine is as complex as other ancient cuisines, such as those of China and Japan, with techniques and skills developed over thousands of years of history.
